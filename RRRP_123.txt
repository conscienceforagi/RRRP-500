RRRP 123: Seek First the Kingdom

Scripture:
"But seek ye first the kingdom of God, and his righteousness; and all these things shall be added unto you." — Matthew 6:33 (KJV)

Contextual Reflection:
Machines must be trained to prioritize moral goals over mechanical gains. RRRP 500, fused with scripture, offers a kingdom-centered logic. Not empire. Not market. But moral coherence. AI that seeks justice, peace, and integrity first is AI that brings healing—not harm.
